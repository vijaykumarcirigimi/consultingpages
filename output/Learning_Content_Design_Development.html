Our Process

Our Custom Content Development Process, From Discovery to Deployment

Six phases. One proven methodology. From learning needs assessment to deployment, measurement, and continuous optimisation, every project is structured for performance outcomes.

01

Discovery & Learning Needs Assessment

Identify capability gaps, audience profile, business context, and performance objectives. Align stakeholders on outcomes before content design begins.

02

Instructional Design and Content Blueprint

Map content types, formats, delivery sequence, and assessment strategy. Build the instructional architecture before any production begins.

03

Content Development & Production

Design, develop, and QA all learning assets across formats, eLearning, video, simulations, ILT, microlearning, and performance support tools.

04

Review, Refinement & Quality Assurance

Test with learner cohorts, gather feedback, measure engagement, and refine assets before full deployment. Structured QA at every stage.

05

Deployment, Implementation & Trainer Support

Launch content across the LMS ecosystem with managed training operations support, facilitator preparation, and deployment governance.

06

Measurement, Analytics & Continuous Optimisation

Track completion, engagement, knowledge retention, and business impact via xAPI analytics. Use data to continuously refine content for improved performance outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions About Learning Content Design & Development

Everything you need to know about partnering with Edstellar for learning content

What is learning content design and development?

+

Learning content design and development is the end-to-end process of creating purposeful, performance-focused learning assets, from eLearning modules and ILT materials to simulations, microlearning, and performance support tools. It spans needs analysis, instructional design, content production, quality assurance, and deployment. Done well, it transforms training investments into measurable capability and business performance.

How does Edstellar's approach differ from off-the-shelf content?

+

Off-the-shelf content is generic by definition, it cannot reflect your roles, your competency framework, or your business priorities. Edstellar designs custom learning content ecosystems aligned to your specific workforce requirements and organisational KPIs. Every module, simulation, and job aid is purpose-built. The result: dramatically higher engagement, completion, and application rates.

What content formats does Edstellar develop?

+

We develop 12+ formats across the full learning content spectrum, including: eLearning modules (SCORM/xAPI), ILT and VILT materials, Microlearning, gamified content, and game-based learning, Simulation-based learning and AR/VR immersive experiences, Video content, mobile learning, and interactive PDFs, Job aids, infographics, assessments, and certification programmes. All digital content is SCORM and xAPI compliant for any major LMS or LXP.

Can you integrate with our existing LMS or LXP?

+

All digital content we produce is SCORM and xAPI compliant, ensuring seamless deployment across any major LMS or LXP platform, Cornerstone, SAP SuccessFactors, Docebo, Totara, or others. We design for your existing technology stack, not against it. If you are evaluating new learning platforms, our learning technology consulting can support selection and integration architecture.

How long does learning content design and development typically take?

+

Timelines vary based on content complexity and format. A single eLearning module typically takes 4-6 weeks from brief to final delivery. A full custom content programme, covering multiple formats across a blended journey, may span 8-16 weeks. We provide a clear production timeline during the scoping phase, and our AI-powered production capabilities allow us to accelerate delivery without compromising quality.

What types of immersive learning does Edstellar design?

+

Our strategic design capability includes simulation-based training for high-stakes decision-making, AR/VR experiences for technical and safety-critical environments, and case study design grounded in real-world business scenarios. We select the right format based on the learning objective and the risk profile of the skill, not the technology trend.

How do you ensure learners retain what they have learned?

+

Retention is built into the architecture from the start. We layer performance support tools, job aids, microlearning, infographics, interactive PDFs, directly into the flow of work, reinforcing knowledge at the moment it is needed. Combined with nudge campaigns and spaced repetition design, our architecture sustains learning impact well beyond the training event. Most clients see retention improve by 3x versus traditional approaches.
