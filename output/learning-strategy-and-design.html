Frequently asked questions on learning strategy design

Everything you need to know about Edstellar's learning strategy consulting services

What is learning strategy design and how does it differ from training delivery?

+

Learning strategy design is the architectural layer above training delivery. Rather than selecting courses, it defines why your organization invests in learning, what capability outcomes to target, how to measure progress against business KPIs, and where to allocate budget for maximum impact. Edstellar's approach ensures training spend is diagnostic-led and outcome-measured — not activity-driven.

How long does a learning strategy engagement typically take?

+

A typical enterprise learning strategy engagement runs 6–12 weeks depending on organizational complexity. This includes stakeholder alignment, diagnostic assessment, strategy architecture, roadmap development, and governance framework design. For organizations requiring immediate direction, Edstellar offers a focused 3-week Learning Strategy Sprint covering priority assessment and a 90-day action plan.

What deliverables does Edstellar produce during a learning strategy engagement?

+

Deliverables typically include a Learning Strategy Blueprint, a prioritized capability roadmap, learning pathway architecture for critical roles, a technology recommendation report, a governance and measurement framework, and an executive summary with ROI projections. Every deliverable is customized to the organization's maturity, budget, and business context.

Can Edstellar also execute the strategy it designs?

+

Yes, and this is a core differentiator. Unlike pure consulting firms that hand over a strategy deck and walk away, Edstellar can execute across training delivery (2,000+ programs), learning architecture (SCORM, gamification, AR/VR), managed training operations, and skills intelligence. Strategy and execution through one partner eliminates the translation gap that causes most learning strategies to fail.

How does Edstellar ensure the strategy is grounded in data, not assumptions?

+

Every strategy engagement begins with diagnostic rigour. Edstellar integrates Talent Diagnostics, psychometric profiling, skills validation, and 360-degree feedback alongside business performance data and stakeholder interviews. This evidence base ensures recommendations target real capability gaps, not perceived ones. The result is a strategy your CHRO and CFO can both defend.

How do you measure the ROI of a learning strategy engagement?

+

Edstellar establishes clear, measurable KPIs at the start of every engagement aligned with your business objectives. Metrics typically include performance improvements against baseline, time-to-productivity for capability-critical roles, leadership pipeline strength scores, training engagement and completion rates, and where applicable, revenue or efficiency gains attributable to capability building. Every engagement concludes with an executive-ready ROI summary.
