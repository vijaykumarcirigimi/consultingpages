Culture Audit: See What's Really Happening Inside Your Organization

A corporate culture audit maps the gap between your organization's stated values and the behaviours, rituals, and power dynamics that actually drive daily decisions. Edstellar's culture transformation consulting practice deploys a multi-method approach, quantitative measurement combined with qualitative depth, to produce a culture health profile with prioritised intervention recommendations your leadership team can act on within weeks.

  • Values-behaviour gap analysis across leadership layers and business units, measuring lived culture vs aspirational values
  • Qualitative deep-dives: focus groups, leadership interviews, artifact review, and behavioural observation
  • Culture health scorecard with dimension-level scoring across psychological safety, innovation climate, accountability, and leadership trust
  • Culture due diligence for M&A integration, post-merger culture integration assessment and change readiness mapping
  • Hybrid work culture and remote team culture health diagnostics for distributed organizations
  • Prioritised action roadmap tied to organisational KPIs and transformation goals

Employee Engagement Surveys & Strategy That Actually Move the Needle

Edstellar's employee engagement consulting practice starts where generic tools stop, designing custom survey instruments and employee listening strategies calibrated to your organisational context, then translating scores into structural interventions that address root causes, not symptoms. Every engagement strategy links directly to business outcomes your CHRO can present to the board, with eNPS tracking and attrition risk analysis built in.

  • Custom employee engagement survey instrument design aligned to organisational maturity, workforce lifecycle stage, and strategic goals
  • Driver analysis isolating structural, managerial, and environmental root causes of disengagement and quiet quitting risk
  • Employee Net Promoter Score (eNPS) baseline, benchmarking, and improvement planning
  • Segmented insights by function, geography, tenure, and management layer, including manager effectiveness scoring
  • Attrition risk analysis identifying which talent segments are most vulnerable to turnover and why
  • Pulse measurement cadence to track impact over 90/180/360 days with engagement action planning
  • Employee wellbeing strategy integration alongside engagement measurement for holistic workforce insight

DEI Diagnostics & Readiness: Build Inclusion on Evidence, Not Assumptions

Edstellar's DEI consulting and DEIB readiness practice assesses six dimensions of organisational readiness, policy infrastructure, leadership capability, hiring equity, promotion patterns, inclusion sentiment, and systemic accessibility, producing a DEI maturity scorecard that tells your leadership team exactly where structural barriers exist and what to address first. Our diversity equity and inclusion consulting is grounded in people analytics, not assumptions.

  • Six-dimension DEIB maturity assessment with scored readiness levels across policy, leadership, hiring, promotion, sentiment, and accessibility
  • Inclusion sentiment survey with demographic intersection analysis and workplace belonging measurement
  • Systemic barrier mapping across hiring equity, development pathways, and advancement patterns
  • Pay equity audit and gender equity gap analysis integrated into the DEI readiness assessment
  • Inclusive leadership capability assessment and unconscious bias diagnostic across management layers
  • ESG people strategy alignment, connecting DEI maturity to social pillar reporting and stakeholder expectations
  • Prioritised DEI intervention roadmap with 90-day, 180-day, and annual milestones tied to measurable outcomes

Culture, Engagement & DEI Consulting, Key Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with Edstellar for culture, engagement, and DEI consulting

What does a culture audit involve?

+

Edstellar's corporate culture audit combines quantitative engagement surveys, qualitative interviews, artifact analysis, and behavioural observation to produce a data-backed view of your organization's lived culture versus its aspirational values. The output includes a culture health scorecard, psychological safety measurement, attrition risk map, and prioritised action roadmap, practical deliverables your leadership team can act on immediately.

How is Edstellar's engagement survey different from off-the-shelf tools?

+

We don't just measure engagement, we build a full employee listening strategy. Our surveys are custom-designed to your organizational context, followed by root-cause analysis, eNPS baseline, attrition risk modelling, and a concrete employee engagement strategy tied to business KPIs, not generic benchmarks. As a specialist employee engagement consulting partner, Edstellar closes the loop from survey to measurable action.

What does a DEI readiness assessment cover?

+

Edstellar's DEI diagnostics evaluate six dimensions: policy infrastructure, inclusive leadership capability, hiring equity, promotion patterns, inclusion sentiment and workplace belonging, and systemic accessibility. The assessment produces a DEIB maturity scorecard with prioritised intervention recommendations, pay equity gap analysis, and ESG people strategy alignment, giving your leadership team a clear starting point for structural change.

Can these services be delivered remotely or in hybrid formats?

+

Yes. Culture audits, employee engagement surveys, and DEI diagnostics can be delivered through a combination of virtual workshops, digital surveys, remote interviews, and on-site immersion sessions across Edstellar's 100+ global delivery locations, giving you full flexibility across geographies, time zones, and hybrid work environments.

How do culture, engagement, and DEI consulting connect to Edstellar's other services?

+

Findings from culture, engagement, and DEI diagnostics directly inform leadership training design, succession planning priorities, competency framework updates, and skills intelligence priorities. Edstellar's integrated consulting model closes the loop from diagnostic insight to measurable intervention, without switching vendors or rebuilding context.
